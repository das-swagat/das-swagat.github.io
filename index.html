About

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Virginia Commonwealth University working at the intersection of artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and cyber-physical systems. My research focuses on scalable, data-driven learning methods for real-world infrastructure and sensing applications.

My work spans self-supervised learning for anomaly detection, AI-enabled security for critical infrastructure, and 3D LiDAR point cloud segmentation for large-scale urban and environmental datasets.

Research Interests

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Cyber-Physical Systems Security
  • Self-Supervised and Representation Learning
  • 3D LiDAR Point Cloud Segmentation
  • Critical Infrastructure Protection
